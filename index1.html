About us

We are a passionate team dedicated to improving our users in a relaxed and pleasant environment.

LET'S FIND THE SAME is very simple, a total of 16 squares, each grid corresponding to a picture.

Players have to do, is to pairs of the same pattern to find out of it, mouse the left key click Spaces, open the box design, if two consecutive click pattern is the same, these two Spaces is always open, all closed, conversely need players continue to click.

The game has a total of two difficulties, easy mode 60 seconds, hard mode 30 seconds, as long as the corresponding time to find all Spaces in the same pattern can pass smoothly. Say so, the actual difficulty of the game is not small, at the beginning, square can open all about 3 seconds, this period of time should grasp remember a few patterns, after which there is no skill, pure test your memory, come on, I wish you early pass advanced difficulty!
